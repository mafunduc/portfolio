Sales Stage Framework
Process architecture for revenue operations

Three opportunity types (Net-New, Expansion, Renewal) all forced through same generic stages, creating forecast confusion. Designed differentiated frameworks aligned to business reality.

Solution Design

  • Net-New Track: 11-stage process with clear qualification gates and progression criteria
  • Expansion Track: 5-stage streamlined process leveraging existing relationships
  • Renewal Track: 6-stage process with automated 120-day triggers
  • Mapped all stages to forecast categories with probability bands
  • Created "stale deal" monitoring for pipeline hygiene

Business Impact: Currently implementing. Projected improvements to forecast accuracy and pipeline velocity through clear progression criteria and automated monitoring.
